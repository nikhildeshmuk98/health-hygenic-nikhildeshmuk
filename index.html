Project Details

Objective

To educate villagers about personal hygiene, handwashing, waste management, and maintaining clean surroundings through interactive sessions and practical demonstrations.

Steps Followed

1

Surveyed local awareness level

Conducted initial surveys to understand the current hygiene practices and awareness levels in the village through door-to-door visits and questionnaires.

2

Prepared posters and leaflets

Created educational materials in the local language with visual aids for better understanding, using culturally appropriate illustrations and simple language.

3

Conducted awareness sessions

Organized interactive sessions in schools and community halls to demonstrate proper hygiene practices through role-plays, demonstrations, and group activities.

4

Distributed hygiene kits

Provided essential hygiene items including soap, sanitizer, toothpaste, and informational pamphlets to villagers with proper usage instructions.

5

Follow-up visit for feedback

Returned to the village to assess the impact of our program and gather feedback for future improvements, measuring behavioral changes and knowledge retention.
